About Me

Jiaqi Zhou

UX Student

University of Michigan

Hi there! Currently, I am a graduate student studying HCI at the University of Michigan.

When I was a kid, I started to learn drawing, which helped me develop an interest in art and design, and also made me become a good observer. In addition, I used to study electronic engineering. In order to combine my interest in design and engineering background, I considered becoming a product designer.

After graduating from university, I interned at TCL and Lantu Tech as a product intern, responsible for product requirement definition and interaction design. The internship experiences confirmed my interest in product design.

In my leisure time, I like drawing and traveling :)
